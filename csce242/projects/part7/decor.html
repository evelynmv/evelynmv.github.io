Entertainment

Entertainment ID: 5625

Mariachi bands perform a diverse array of music that showcases the rich cultural heritage of Mexico. Their repertoire includes rancheras, which are romantic or patriotic ballads that evoke deep emotions. They also feature son jalisciense, a lively regional folk style that hails from Jalisco. In addition to these traditional pieces, mariachi bands embrace boleros and cumbias, blending them seamlessly with modern pop covers, all interpreted in their distinctive mariachi style. This variety ensures that their performances resonate with a wide audience, celebrating both the old and the new.

$800 - $2,000+

Entertainment ID: 1651

A party LED robot, also known as a "LED robot dancer" or "robot show," is an eye-catching performer in a costume covered with vibrant LED lights, often several feet tall. They captivate audiences with high-energy dance routines, synchronized lights, lasers, and smoke effects, sometimes using CO₂ guns for dramatic bursts of fog. Popular at quinceañeras, weddings, and nightclubs, these robots create an unforgettable wow factor that enhances the energy of any event.

$400 – $700
